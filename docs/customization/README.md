# Customizing Your Linux System

One of the most appealing aspects of Linux is the unparalleled freedom to customize nearly every part of your operating system. From changing the look and feel of your desktop to tweaking command-line prompts and system behaviors, Linux offers endless possibilities to make your system truly your own.

This section provides guides on how to personalize your Linux experience.
